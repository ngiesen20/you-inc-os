FAQ

Common questions

Is this really a one-time payment?▼
Yes. $10 once. You get permanent access with no recurring fees, ever. All future updates are included.
Where is my data stored?▼
In your own private Supabase database. We cannot see it. Finance data is kept local on your device only — we strongly recommend never logging real financial numbers in a cloud tool.
Can I use this on my phone?▼
Yes. Fully mobile-responsive. Bookmark the URL on your home screen and it works like a native app. All data syncs instantly across devices.
How much time does this actually take?▼
10 minutes daily (5 morning, 5 evening). 60 minutes Sunday. 90 minutes monthly. The system is designed specifically to survive 100-hour IB weeks — it has a Deal Sprint mode that reduces it to 3 inputs per day.
I'm not in finance — is this still for me?▼
The system was built with IB in mind but the architecture works for any high-performer with extreme time constraints — consultants, founders, pre-MBA professionals, anyone building toward dynasty-level outcomes.
What happens after I pay?▼
You're redirected back here, create an account, complete a 5-minute onboarding, and your OS is live. Access is instant.
